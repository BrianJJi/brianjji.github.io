THE INDEX CARD


Rule #1: Aim to save 50% or more of your after-tax income. Invest the rest wisely.

Rule #2: Avoid consumer debt and live frugally. Pay your credit card balance in full every month.

Rule #3: Max out your 401K, TFSA and other tax-advantaged savings accounts.

Rule #4: Learn basic investment strategies with options: covered calls, cash-secured puts + LEAPS.

Rule #5: When you are financially ready, buy a house and house hack.

Rule #6: Make your financial advisor commit to the fiduciary standard.

Rule #7: Start a side hustle, even if it's not your fulltime gig.

Rule #8: Get insurance and make sure you are protected.

Rule #9: Do what you can to support the social safety net.

Rule #10: Remember the index card.
